Venue Information

The Jkuat Conference will take place at the Jkuat University campus in Juja.

Main Hall

The Main Hall is located in the central building of the campus and will host the keynote and plenary sessions. It is easily accessible from the main entrance of the campus.

Room 101

Room 101 is located in the east wing of the campus and will host the "Machine Learning for Beginners" and "Cloud Computing with AWS" sessions. It is a short walk from the Main Hall.

Room 102

Room 102 is located in the west wing of the campus and will host the "Front-end Development with React" and "Cybersecurity in the Modern Era" sessions. It is accessible via the campus courtyard.
